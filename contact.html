About Our Group

The LangHealth Group meets virtually for one hour, twice a month, to discuss recent developments in language and health research, share knowledge dissemination opportunities, and present ongoing projects.

Presentations and discussions are held in both French and English to reflect the group's bilingual nature.

The group is open to the public. To be added to our mailing list, please contact us at the email address above.
